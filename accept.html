High Standards

We have several distinct areas we focus on: Women's and Men's Fashion and Accessories, Fine Jewelry, Watches, Art, and Home. These categories offer an extensive selection of high-end luxury items, but we adhere to a guideline of established standards, regarding what we accept for consignment.

Jewelry and Watches are evaluate on a piece-by-piece basis, with only a few specific acceptance restrictions. If you are near one of our Valuation Offices, specializing in valuations for fine jewelry and watches, we encourage scheduling an appointment today.

Since Home and Art items usually require special care and handling, please reach out to us first.

Designer Brands

For Women's and Men's fashion, we accept items from over 350 designer brands found on our Designer List, and we're pleased to consider your luxury clothing, shoes, bags, belts, wallets, accessories and more for consignment. Note that
certain brands have limitations on the types of items accepted.

Also, as you are deciding on what to consign, understand that some designer and luxury items sell quicker than others - at any given time - depending on factors such as current customer demand, fashion trends, seasonality, and an individual item's characteristics and condition.

If you have specific item or brand-related questions, please contact our team.

Incredibly good looking

Naturally, everyone expects to receive something wonderful when they choose to buy from us, so we have several condition standards in place. We only accept items which could be considered pristine, excellent, or in very good condition.

We also like to keep it current, so apparel and shoes must not date back more than 10-12 years. Vintage is generally not accepted unless it qualifies as a remarkable piece. Very few exceptions are made.
