Top questions about what to expect when consigning
Appointments:

How long is an average in-home appointment? How should I prepare?

A white glove in-home appointment can last for 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how many items you have ready for our Luxury Manager to evaluate. As a courtesy, please buffer your appointment time by an extra 15 minutes. In preparation, look up similar items to your own and see prices of previously recently sold items.

Pricing:

Do any items qualify for a price approval request? If so, which ones and what is the process like?

Items valued at $995+ are elligible for a “Price Approval” before it goes on sale. Furs and exotic skins may also qualify. If you have pricing sensitivity around your goods, ask your Luxury Manager or our team about this practice. Understand that the communication exchange about your item may take extra time and could delay its official launch to the site.

Sales:

How will I know when my items have been accepted and are officially on sale?

You will receive an email to notify you about which items have been accepted and which ones will be returned to you (including reasons.) Please check on your My Sales page periodically to see status updates regarding processing and various sale statuses.
