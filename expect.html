Receiving & Processing

We communicate each step of the way. You'll know via email when your items have been received and are in process. Our trained in-house experts will take the time to evaluate and describe your items. A follow-up email will let you know which have been accepted for sale.

Standards and criteria are taken into account when we accept and price items. Keep in mind, some may take longer to sell than others. This can depend on several factors at any given time - current fashion trends, shopper demand, condition, and item characteristics.

Let the sales begin

Once your items have been accepted and are available for sale, you can follow along with updates on your My Sales page. Check on the status of items, your current commission rate, and commission values per item. Everything you need to know is in one place.

Each month we run a different promotional incentive for first time Consignors. Once you've qualified, you'll receive details about how and when you'll receive it. Mid-month, commission payments go out for sales during the prior month, and as your preferred method of payment.

It's truly addictive!

If you believe shopping on The RealReal is addictive, you should experience how addictive consigning with us can actually be! Turn those clothes, bags, shoes, jewelry, and accessories in cash or site credit today.

Our strategic goal to sell well starts with launching items at their highest possible sale price within the first 30 days. You'll then get the best value when fast sales happen. In traditional consignment, the typical pay out is a 50-60% commission. At The RealReal, Consignors can earn up to 70% commission - the more you sell, the more you earn!

Top questions
about what to expect when consigning
Appointments:

How long is an average in-home appointment? How should I prepare?

A white glove in-home appointment can last for 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how many items you have ready for our Luxury Manager to evaluate. As a courtesy, please buffer your appointment time by an extra 15 minutes. In preparation, look up similar items to your own and see prices of previously recently sold items.

Pricing:

Do any items qualify for a price approval request? If so, which ones and what is the process like?

Items valued at $995+ are elligible for a “Price Approval” before it goes on sale. Furs and exotic skins may also qualify. If you have pricing sensitivity around your goods, ask your Luxury Manager or our team about this practice. Understand that the communication exchange about your item may take extra time and could delay its official launch to the site.

Sales:

How will I know when my items have been accepted and are officially on sale?

You will receive an email to notify you about which items have been accepted and which ones will be returned to you (including reasons.) Please check on your My Sales page periodically to see status updates regarding processing and various sale statuses.
