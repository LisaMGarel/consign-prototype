Evaluation & Merchandising

We take on all the effort to sell for you, and our in-house
team of trained specialists is best-suited for the task. Once your consignment has been received, the team considers items in terms of their secondary market value and what our audience finds desirable.

Since there is an evergreen 20% off promotion, we initially set merchandise at their highest sale price. Your pieces will also be placed in strategic flash sales over time to be front of millions of the right shoppers at the right time. We look forward to making fast sales happen for you.

Authentication & Pricing

We are committed to a promise of authenticity because we truly respect the brands we represent and value customer satisfaction. Every day our trained experts - authenticators, horologists, gemologists, and art curators - thoroughly inspect the quality of thousands of incoming items and fully confirm their authenticity.

We set market-appropriate prices based on an item's
individual characteristics.It's in the Consignor's and our interest to value goods at their highest possible TRR list price, so we can both earn the best commission value.

Customer Care & Delivery

Not only does your consignment receive the end-to-end special treatment of individual evaluation, professional photography, copywriting, valuation, pricing, and merchandising, our team takes care of all customer needs throughout the entire sale process and purchase fulfillment.

the logistics! We make the sale happen and fully deliver on it for you.
