White glove service

Would you appreciate consignment with a personal and attentive touch in your home? We offer exclusive "white glove" service coverage in many major cities.

If you have several high-end items to consign (ideally, over 10 pieces on our accepted Designers List), upon your request, we will send a high-end luxury goods expert - a Luxury Manager - to your home to evaluate and do a pick-up. After, our internal team will and evaluate and process your consignment within the week it has been received.
